About Snek
SNEK is a deflationary memecoin launched on Cardano. It has become a cultural movement and an icon in the web3 world, representing fun and innovation.
SNEK is the most traded token on Cardano and a unifying force within the ecosystem. It brings excitement to its community and plays a leading role in making the crypto space accessible, engaging, and meaningful. With its roots in internet culture and fueled by a dedicated online community, Snek serves as a vital bridge for web3 onboarding.
Like other popular memecoins, such as Pepe, Bonk, Shiba Inu, and Dogecoin, SNEK has established itself as one of the top meme-based community-driven cryptocurrencies. It pushes the limits of what a memecoin can achieve, with a vision to bring social and decentralized finance within and beyond the Cardano ecosystem.

50% - Presale holders
50% of the token supply is being distributed amongst presale participants. Presale was open to the public with no prior qualifications. This will be based on a snapshot of funds sent, and airdropped via a third party distribution service. The SNEK early contributors did not benefit at all from this sale.
40% - Initial liquidity
40% of the token supply has been allocated towards initial liquidity distribution, this is being used in SundaeSwap and Minswap. LP tokens are being stored in a multisig wallet for safety.
2% - Marketing
2% of the token supply is being used for marketing promotions, community airdrops, contests, events, further giveaways and incentives for the promotion of SNEK. 1% was distributed within the first month.
5% - Utility
5% of the token supply is being used towards the SNEK ecosystem, DeFi applications, partnerships and project developments.
3% - Reserve
3% of the token supply is reserved for exchange listings, additional liquidity and expansion opportunities.
